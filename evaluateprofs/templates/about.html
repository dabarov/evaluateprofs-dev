About us

My name is Aldiyar and thank you for using this Website.

Why?

Each semester starts with the registration of subjects. It is a struggle to choose the right professor. Here we come :). On our website you can see others opinion on your potential professors.

In the end of each semester, students give feedback to the schools. However, they are not public and we do not have access to them. It is unclear what is done with our feedback. But here you can leave you honest opinion and may be it will make an effect.
